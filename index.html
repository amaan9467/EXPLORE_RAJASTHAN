The Pink City

Jaipur

Step into the royal capital of Rajasthan, where pink-hued palaces and magnificent forts tell tales of Rajput grandeur. Jaipur seamlessly blends ancient heritage with modern vibrancy, offering an enchanting experience of royal luxury and cultural richness.

  • Magnificent Hawa Mahal - Palace of Winds
  • Imposing Amber Fort with elephant rides
  • Bustling bazaars of Johari and Bapu
  • UNESCO World Heritage City status
  • Luxurious heritage hotels and palaces
Explore Jaipur
Venice of the East

Udaipur

Discover the most romantic city of India, where pristine lakes reflect magnificent palaces and the Aravalli hills provide a stunning backdrop. Udaipur's ethereal beauty and royal legacy create an atmosphere of timeless romance and regal splendor.

  • Floating City Palace on Lake Pichola
  • Romantic boat rides at sunset
  • Intricate Jagdish Temple architecture
  • Luxury lake-facing heritage resorts
  • Vibrant traditional puppet shows
Explore Udaipur
The Blue City

Jodhpur

Immerse yourself in the azure charm of Jodhpur, where blue-painted houses cascade beneath the mighty Mehrangarh Fort. This desert citadel offers breathtaking panoramic views and showcases the finest examples of Rajputana architecture and warrior heritage.

  • Towering Mehrangarh Fort - one of India's largest
  • Blue-painted old city streets
  • Magnificent Umaid Bhawan Palace
  • Authentic Rajasthani cuisine and sweets
  • Traditional handicrafts and textiles
Explore Jodhpur
The Golden City

Jaisalmer

Experience the magic of the Thar Desert in Jaisalmer, where golden sandstone architecture glows like a mirage against endless sand dunes. This ancient trading post offers camel safaris, desert camping, and unforgettable sunset experiences in the heart of the desert.

  • Living fort with shops and hotels inside
  • Camel safaris on Sam Sand Dunes
  • Intricately carved Jain temples
  • Desert camping under starlit skies
  • Traditional folk music and dance performances
Explore Jaisalmer
The Camel City

Bikaner

Discover the desert jewel of Bikaner, famous for its magnificent forts, unique camel research facilities, and mouth-watering snacks. This lesser-explored gem offers authentic desert culture, stunning architecture, and the famous Karni Mata Temple with its revered rats.

  • Imposing Junagarh Fort with pristine condition
  • National Research Centre on Camel
  • Famous Bikaneri bhujia and sweets
  • Unique Karni Mata Temple (Rat Temple)
  • Traditional haveli architecture
Explore Bikaner
